EVENT-uality

This app allows users to have the calendar, weather, and local events all in one place. With one click, the user can add local events onto their calendar or input their own event into the calendar. Multiplayer Rock, Paper, Scissor

Two players can play at a time in this online head-to-head rock, paper, scissor game. At the initial sign on screen, the green button will activate once two players have logged onto the website. Then, both players can verse each other in as many games as they wish. An added bonus is the "chatter box" so players can talk to one another during the game.
